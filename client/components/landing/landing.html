Research You Want

KickStudy is a way for you to get the research you want.


How It Works:
  1. Independent researchers post proposed studies on the site and ask for funding.
  2. You search KickStudy for proposed studies that interest you.
  3. Encourage resarchers to complete the study byvfunding the study.
  4. Get the research that is important to you!
Unbiased

Many research studies are funded by organizations that have a financial interest in the topic. This makes it difficult to determine if the study is truely unbiased.


With so much knowledge available online, it's difficult to determine the accuracy of information.


Through crowdfunding, regular people are empowered to support scientific research that is important to them.


You can feel confident that the scientific studies that impact you are unbiased.
